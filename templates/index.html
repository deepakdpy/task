ConnecTheShelf

ConnecTheShelf is book rental and selling service offered by an opensource foundation. We offer you your favourite books and minimum load to your pocket. Whether renting or selling our prime goal is to provide you the best services at the most affordable services. Connect with us to enjoy your services.

Our Services

Buy book

Buy new and old books at very affordable price. If not available then make a request. We'll provide that at no extra cost.

Rent

Book on rent to rant. We also have a rental service where you can rent a book and then return after the specified time.

Plans

There are some extra benefits which you would get on taking our membership plans. Please have a look at it.

Donate a book

If you have a book which you might not need in future then donate it to us and we would give it to someone who needs it. Click to read more

Get in connection

You can contact us whenever you face any problem or you have any kind of doubt or query. We would be glad to resolve your queries and problems.

Events

ConnecTheShelf keeps on celebrating festivals. You can always enjoy with us by having a look at event section and having amazing features and discounts.
